$$\begin{eqnarray}
条件: \qquad 
&①& \quad 正项级数\sum\limits^{\infty}_{n=1}u_{n}\\
&②& \quad u_{n} > 0 \\
&③& \quad \lim_{n \rightarrow + \infty} \frac{u_{n+1}}{u_{n}}=l
\end{eqnarray}$$

$$\Rightarrow$$

$$\begin{eqnarray}
&Ⅰ& \quad l<1 \Rightarrow \sum\limits^{\infty}_{n=1}u_{n}收敛 \\
&Ⅱ& \quad l>1 \Rightarrow \sum\limits^{\infty}_{n=1}u_{n}发散
\end{eqnarray}$$
